Our Mission

YDOR focuses on Small and Medium-sized Enterprises. We build private, GDPR-compliant AI systems that automate the repetitive operational work. Everything runs on your own infrastructure, with ROI measurable in months. Our mission is to help your team pick the right tools, use them well, and customize systems that fit how you already operate.

How we work

01

Free consultation

A 30-minute call. We hear what’s slowing your team down and tell you honestly whether AI is the right tool for it.

02

Free scoping

If there’s a fit, we map the workflow, the data, and the realistic ROI. You get a concrete proposal. No commitment.

03

Build

We install the system inside your infrastructure, hand over ownership, and stay on hand while your team gets comfortable with it.

Why this approach

Private & on-prem

The system runs on your infrastructure. Data never leaves your environment.

GDPR-compliant by design

Frequently asked

Is our data safe? +

Yes. The system runs inside your infrastructure. No customer or operational data is sent to third parties unless you explicitly enable it.

How long until something is running? +

The free scoping gives you a concrete timeline before any commitment. Typical first deployments land in 4–8 weeks.

What does it cost? +

The consultation and scoping are free. The build cost depends on scope and is fixed before work starts; no open-ended retainers.

What if it doesn’t work? +

We scope before we build. If the scoping shows the ROI isn’t there, we tell you and don’t take the project. Better for both sides.
